Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Your privacy is important to us. MindMirror is designed with your privacy in mind.

For detailed information about how we handle data (even though this app primarily uses local storage and does not collect personal data on servers), please review our full Privacy Policy hosted by TermsFeed:

View Our Full Privacy Policy

Key points regarding this MindMirror app specifically:

  • Local Storage: All your mood entries and notes are stored locally in your browser's localStorage. This data remains on your device.
  • No Server Collection: We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal mood data on any external servers through the core functionality of this app.
  • Data Control: You can clear your browser's cache or site data for MindMirror to erase your stored mood history at any time.

The linked Privacy Policy provides comprehensive details applicable to online services. For this particular app, the local storage aspect means your personal mood data doesn't leave your device through our direct actions.
